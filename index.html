Requirements

About the community
My main goal with this guild is to make a friendly sweaty bedwars community, that means that we will make: Events, parties (like lobby 1), gvg(guild vs guild), ranked, and much more!
Applications

About the Ranks in the Guild
You might be wondering, "How are we gonna get invitedif you don't know if we are actually good" That's where the Member role and the Trusted role comes in. Member role means that you meet requirements. Trusted role means that you've 1v1 a staff member and the staff promoted you, because he thinks that you are good enough.
